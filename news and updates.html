News & Updates

Annual Sports Meet 2026

The Annual Sports Meet will be held on 15th July 2026 at the school playground. This event is one of the most exciting activities in the school calendar, where students from all grades participate in various sports such as running races, long jump, high jump, cricket, volleyball, and relay races. The main aim of the event is to promote physical fitness, teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship among students. Teachers and parents are also invited to encourage the participants. Winners will be awarded certificates and trophies. All students are encouraged to actively participate and support their classmates during this important school event.

Science Fair 2026

The Science Exhibition will be organized on 10th August 2026 in the school main hall. Students from different grades will present their science projects, experiments, and innovative ideas related to physics, chemistry, biology, and technology. This event helps students develop creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills. It also encourages them to apply classroom knowledge in practical situations. Judges will evaluate the projects based on creativity, explanation, and usefulness. Parents and visitors are welcome to attend and support the students. The exhibition aims to inspire young minds to explore science and develop an interest in future scientific careers.

Computer Lab Upgrade

The school has recently upgraded its computer laboratory with modern computers, updated software, and high-speed internet access. This improvement aims to provide students with better opportunities to learn information technology and develop digital skills. The new lab allows students to practice programming, research online, and improve their knowledge of modern technology. Teachers will also use the lab for interactive lessons and practical demonstrations. This upgrade supports the school’s goal of providing a high-quality education in the digital age. Students are expected to use the lab responsibly and make the best use of the available resources for their studies

English Day Celebration

English Day will be celebrated on 5th September 2026 at the school auditorium. This special event is organized to improve students’ English speaking, writing, and communication skills. Students will participate in various activities such as speeches, debates, drama performances, poetry recitations, and storytelling. The event helps build confidence and encourages students to express their ideas in English. Teachers will guide students in preparing their performances. Parents are also invited to enjoy the program and support the students. English Day plays an important role in developing language skills and motivating students to use English in their daily communication.
